What is the Digital Village

The Digital Village is more than just an online community, it's your safe space to connect, learn, and grow alongside fellow advocates fighting cyberbullying.

Safe Community
  • Connect with verified members who share your commitment to online kindness
  • Moderated discussions ensure respectful, harassment-free interactions
  • Anonymous participation options for those who need extra privacy
Exclusive Learning
  • Access member-only workshops, webinars, and training sessions
  • Early access to new resources and campaign materials
  • Personalized learning paths based on your interests and experience
Peer Support Network
  • Find mentors and mentees through our matching program
  • 24/7 peer support for those experiencing online harassment
  • Share experiences and healing journeys in a supportive environment
Amplify Your Impact
  • Collaborate on advocacy projects and policy campaigns
  • Lead local chapters and organize community events
  • Recognition system that celebrates your contributions
