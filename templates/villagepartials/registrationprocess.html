Registration Process

Step 1: Create Your Profile
  • Basic information (Name, Email, University/Organization)
  • Location (County/City for local connections)
  • Interests and advocacy areas
  • Profile photo (optional)
Step 2: Community Guidelines Agreement
  • Commit to respectful communication
  • Agree to support fellow members
  • Understand confidentiality expectations
  • Accept mentorship opportunities
Step 3: Choose Your Path
  • Survivor Support: Connect with others who've experienced cyberbullying
  • Advocate Network: Join policy and platform accountability efforts
  • Educator Hub: Share knowledge and create educational content
  • Creative Collective: Use art, writing, and media for awareness
